Quinoa Chili One Pot

Description

One pot of quinoa, grounded pork, salsa, shredded cheese, corn, and diced carrots, onions and celery.

Ingredients

  • Grounded pork, 1 lb
  • Quinoa, 1 cup
  • Salsa, 1 1/2 cup
  • Chili diced vegetables, 2 cups
  • Shredded cheddar, 1 1/2 cup
  • Chicken stock, 2 cup
  • Chili seasoning, 1 tbs

Steps

  1. In a oven-safe skillet, heat up some olive oil on medium heat. Cook the pork for 5-6 minutes.
  2. Add the seasoning, then the quinoa, salsa, vegetables and chicken stock. Stir. Bring to a boil, cover and simmer for 10-12 minutes, until the quinoa is cooked.
  3. Garnish with shredded cheddar. Gratin in the oven for 2-3 minutes.
